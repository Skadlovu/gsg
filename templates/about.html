About Us

The roots hidden underneath the soil.

The Company

The Green Soil Group is an agricultural industrial centre that focuses on the preservation of seeds, agricultural research and services, and also producing and supplying herbal and organic food.

We work alongside farmers, producers, manufacturers,retailers, governments and other orgarnisations to fulfil our purpose.

We offer inputs, expertise and risk management tools to farmers small and large, helping them boost their productivity and income, we purchase their crops and animals and bring them to a global market.

We process a wide range of agricultural products into end products,both for business and consumer use, delivered through an efficient and effective transport system to the various places of use.

Mission

Our mission is to join with community leaders,non-profits and others to enrich the places where we live and work, building a strong, and sustainable future for agriculture

Vision

To nourish the agriculture industry, in a responsible and sustainable manner, together; create efficiencies, develop innovations and help communities

Objective

Calcify the Southern African agricultural industry and increase its capacity to compete on the global market

Maximise opportunities for research, development and innovation in nature-based products and services
